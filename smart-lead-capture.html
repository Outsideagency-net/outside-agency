How It Works

Smarter Than a Popup. Simpler Than You'd Expect.

01

Intent Detection

Visitor lands and shows an intent signal — scroll depth, time on page, or cursor moving toward the exit.

02

Activation

Smart Lead Capture activates within 5 seconds with a conversion-optimized offer matched to the page.

03

Re-Engagement

If dismissed, the system re-engages after 20 seconds with a softer, lower-commitment prompt.

Once the visitor converts to a lead, the popup stops completely. No harassing confirmed leads.
